MINT

Discourse Diversity in Multi-Turn Empathic Dialogue

LLMs often get stuck using the same empathy tactic turn after turn. MINT encourages more varied, human sounding support while preserving empathy quality.

Why MINT?

Vanilla LLM
Turn 1
"I've been feeling really overwhelmed at work lately. My manager keeps piling on new projects and I don't know how to say no."
"It sounds like you're really struggling with the workload right now. That must be incredibly stressful, and your feelings are completely valid."
Paraphrasing Validation
Turn 2
"Yeah, I think part of it is that I'm afraid of being seen as incompetent. Like if I push back, they'll think I can't handle it."
"I hear you — it sounds like the fear of being judged is making it even harder. It's completely understandable to feel that way."
Paraphrasing Validation
Turn 3
"I just don't know where to start. Everything feels urgent and I can't prioritize."
"It sounds like you're dealing with a lot of pressure from all directions. That's a really tough spot to be in, and it makes sense you'd feel stuck."

MINT
Turn 1
"I've been feeling really overwhelmed at work lately. My manager keeps piling on new projects and I don't know how to say no."
"That sounds really stressful. What kinds of projects are being added — are they related to your main role, or are they coming from different directions?"
Validation Questioning
Turn 2
"Yeah, I think part of it is that I'm afraid of being seen as incompetent. Like if I push back, they'll think I can't handle it."
"That fear makes a lot of sense, but pushing back on scope is actually a sign of professional maturity, not weakness. The strongest people I know are the ones who set clear boundaries."
Reappraisal Empowerment
Turn 3
"I just don't know where to start. Everything feels urgent and I can't prioritize."
"One thing that might help: pick just one task that you can finish today, even if it's small. Getting a quick win can break that frozen feeling."
Advice Empowerment

MINT achieves the best balance of empathy and diversity across both model sizes.
